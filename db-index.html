About

Sri Lanka Nidahas Sewaka Sangamaya Central Eletracity Board

  • reg.No:
  • 3030

Our organization was established in 1994 in the Ceylon Electricity Board under the name of CEB Independent Employees Union. We are fortunate to be one of the oldest and foremost trade unions established by the Ceylon Electricity Board. It is always our intention and intention to work for the well-being of our members and the organization. We are committed to it in the past and in the present and we have a responsibility to do so in the future.
